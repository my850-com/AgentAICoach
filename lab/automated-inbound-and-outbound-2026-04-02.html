Automated Inbound and Outbound Call Agent

Deployed

One of our most requested capabilities from customers has been automated prospecting. Today we tested a simple prospecting engine that had to meet three critical requirements.

Our Requirements

  1. Simple setup — Easy to navigate UI/dashboard without technical expertise
  2. Free to test — No upfront costs for initial setup and testing
  3. No external telephony — No Twilio or complex phone infrastructure required

The Solution: SynthFlow AI

While there were several options available, SynthFlow AI emerged as the best fit for our requirements and client needs. We wanted something simple and easy to implement — not enterprise-grade complexity.

We prioritized tools that wouldn't overwhelm our real estate, investing, and lending clients during implementation.

Costs & Timeline

Setup Phase ~$25 Phone number purchase + testing minutes
Initial Setup Easy Very straightforward dashboard
Optimization Moderate Required tweaking prompts and flow

The Build Process

1. Phone Number & Agent Setup

Purchased a local number through SynthFlow's interface. The agent creation process was intuitive — we defined the persona, script flow, and response patterns directly in the dashboard.

2. Integration Testing

We ran multiple test calls to refine the conversation flow. The AI needed adjustment for:

  • Natural pause timing between sentences
  • Handling objections ("No thanks", "Not interested")
  • Email capture and confirmation flow
  • Time zone clarification

3. Data Pipeline (Google Sheets)

For call data extraction, we built a custom Google Apps Script webhook that receives data from SynthFlow's webhooks and logs:

  • Caller name and phone number
  • Call duration and transcript
  • AI analysis (interested vs. declined)
  • Full conversation summary

This required some trial and error — the payload structure was different than documented, so we had to debug the actual JSON being sent.

The Results

✅ Fully Functional

  • Inbound call handling with voicemail
  • Outbound prospecting with natural conversation flow
  • Automatic email capture and calendar booking
  • Real-time data logging to Google Sheets
  • Email notifications for team on new calls
