Automated inbound and outbound call agent

One of our most requested capabilities requested by customers has been automated prospecting. Today we are testing a simple prospecting engine which had to meet three requirements. 1. It must be a simple to set up and move through the user interface or dashboard. 2. It must be free to test and set up. 3. It must not require an outside telephonic set up like twillow. While there were a handful of options, all things in balance, SynthFlow ai seemed meet our initial requirements the best. We want something simple and easy to implement for our clients. This was a successful test with one caution. We noticed our set up phase cost us about $25 dollars. This included phone number purchase and system minutes for testing and optimization. The initial set up was extremely easy. The optimization on the other hand took a little work to tweek just right. Check our results by visiting our home page. We have a sample ai agent ready to chat with you. Another area that can be handled multiple ways is in regard to the data harvested from the calls. We chose to create google sheet web hooks which also took some trial and error. Our finished test product was a fully functional outbound and inbound call agent.
