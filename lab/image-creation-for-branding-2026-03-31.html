Image creation for branding

One of the most important aspects of Social media is branding. Especially for agents and lenders. In a way, and agents brand is their business. Many AI's so far have challenges with branding images. Primarily when there is text or logo art involved. Our "Old School" free image creator has always been PhotoScape. While we've tried many online editors, nothing beats the convenience of a desktop app. One thing that would be more convenient would be to vibe code logo and social media art. As of today we don't have a working solution for this. Todays test is figma.com. It passes the first test, there is a free version for limited images. It's not AI or prompt driven either. What it is, a straight forward simple way to make logo type images fast and with a simple to use interface. Best of all there are many social media templates available. Why that matters? Each Social media platform has different size requirements and image safe zone. This feature alone has saved us a bunch of time when setting up complete automation and marketing campaigns.

The Problem

We are looking for a fully AI driven image creation platform capable of producing correctly sized social media channel artwork including text.

The Solution

At the moment we have not found the solution but continue to use our free work arounds. PhotoScape on our desk top and figma.com as our online.

Tools Used

  • figma.com and PhotoScape
