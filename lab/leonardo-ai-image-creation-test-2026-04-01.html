Leonardo AI Image Creation Test

Tested Leonardo.ai to create a simple set of images for a YouTube channel using this prompt: "create all necessary art work for my YouTube channel making sure it fits with YouTube recommended sized and banner guides" and the end result of my prompt was this error message: "Failed Generation Your request did not meet content safety guidelines. Your tokens have been credited back to your account"

The Problem

A quick and easy way to generate images. Specifically YouTube channel art.

The Solution

While Leonardo.ai shows promise and has a rich UI/Dashboard making for an easy experience. The lack of output for what seems like a reasonably easy task made me lose interest in this AI very fast. Research into the error message suggested this: This message means Leonardo.AI detected that your prompt or input image violated their safety, ethical, or intellectual property guidelines, preventing the image from being generated. With an easy task like this getting "flagged" it looks like Leonardo.ai (at least the free tier) may not be a stable and quick choice for real estate image creation.
